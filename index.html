CM Electric
CM Electric was a web-based system designed to streamline the operations of an electrical services company. It allowed customers to book services, while employees could manage and track job assignments. The system provided a central hub for the manager to oversee all activities, ensuring efficient scheduling, task management, and service delivery. It consisted of three main components:


1. Client Site: Customers could browse available services, request bookings, and track the status of their requests. They could also view their quotations, decide on a suitable booking date, and confirm their appointments, offering a seamless experience for managing their service needs.


2. Technician App: This mobile app allowed technicians to view and manage their assigned jobs. They could update the status of tasks, mark jobs as complete, and communicate with the manager, ensuring smooth operations in the field.

3. Manager (Admin) Side: The admin interface gave the manager full control over the system, allowing them to:

- Change prices and add new services to the offerings.

- View analytics and track company performance.

- Generate quotations and invoices for customers.

- Add new technicians to the system and manage their job assignments.

- Manage the company’s inventory, ensuring stock levels were always updated.

This system streamlined operations for customers, technicians, and the manager, improving efficiency, task management, and overall service delivery.
